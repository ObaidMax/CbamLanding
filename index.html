Frequently asked

Clear answers,
before the demo.

Does Landed Carbon replace official CBAM reporting?

No. It is a preparation and decision-support platform. Official declarations, verification and legal responsibility remain with the relevant parties.

Where does AI add value?

AI reads and structures unstandardised business documents, highlights absent information and prepares focused follow-up actions.

Why start with aluminium?

A narrow aluminium use case makes the workflow easier to test, explain and improve before the platform expands to additional products or sectors.

Can the dashboard work without AI?

Yes. The exposure model remains transparent and editable. AI improves the upstream collection and readiness process rather than hiding the calculations.
